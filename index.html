Our Church, Our Family

8th Anniversary

Welcome to The Blessing of God International Church

We are a vibrant, Spirit-filled community of believers committed to worship, prayer, teaching, and experiencing the miraculous power of God. Our church is a place where lives are transformed, families are restored, and destinies are fulfilled through the Word of God.

Under anointed leadership, we pursue spiritual growth, deep fellowship, and a closer walk with the Lord. Whether you're seeking healing, deliverance, or simply a place to call home — you belong here.
